The AI Process

From Upload to Transaction
in Four Steps

Our AI pipeline classifies waste, assesses quality, and recommends fair prices — all from a single photo upload.

1
📸

Upload Image

Seller photographs and uploads their waste with basic details like quantity and location.

2
🤖

AI Classification

YOLOv11+ and TensorFlow models identify waste type (plastic, metal, paper, organic, e-waste).

3
⭐

Quality Score

OpenCV analyses colour, texture, and contamination patterns to generate a quality grade.

4
💰

Price Prediction

Market-based pricing engine suggests a fair price range. Listing goes live for buyers.

🧠

Waste Classification Model

Uses YOLOv11+ and image recognition to identify plastic, paper, metal, organic waste, and e-waste from photos with high accuracy.

🔍

Quality Assessment Model

OpenCV and Keras analyse colour, texture, and contamination patterns to produce a quality score (A–D) for each waste batch.

📊

Price Recommendation System

Combines waste category, quality score, quantity, and real-time local market data to suggest a fair and transparent price band.
